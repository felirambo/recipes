Easy Pizza Dough

Description

Easy homemade pizza dough for when you don't want to wait for the dough to rise. You just mix it and allow it to rest for 5 minutes and then it's ready to go! It yields a soft, chewy crust.

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Bread Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix everything till you get a dough
  2. Let the dough come to room temperature (about 5 minutes)
  3. Prepare your surface with oil
  4. Shape the dough with a rolling pin or pat it with your hands
