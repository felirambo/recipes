Peanut Pancake

Description

A pancake with peanut butter as it's topping.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Wheat Flour
  • Butter
  • Peanut Butter
  • Honey

Steps

  1. Mix the eggs with wheat flour in a bowl
  2. Throw some butter into a pan and let it melt
  3. Throw the dough you created into pan and let it cook
  4. When it's cooked, tops the pancake with peanut butter and throws honey to finish
