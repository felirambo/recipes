Beans and Rice

Description

Beans and Rice, a dish full of good protein and carbohydrates.

Ingredients

  • Beans
  • Rice
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Put some olive oil on the pan
  2. Thrown in the rice, salt and mix
  3. Throw two times the rice volume as water
  4. Wait till all the water dry
  5. Put the beans on water and let it cook
